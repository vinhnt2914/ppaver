How it work:

The website concept will be drag and drop. Basically, each item will be embedded with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript code. By dragging items onto the workspace, our system will automatically add those codes into the code space. After finishing the website, users can copy the code for their use. The real-time tutoring is limited to a few templates only, so they are pre-made. The “Make money from your template” function is very simple, after users click on the share button, the template (code included) will be public across our website, and will also be sent to us to evaluate. If all requirements are matched, we will send them an email informing them that their templates are qualified and ask whether they wanted to put a price on their template or not. They will be selling their work for our currency, they can choose whether to buy other components, effects and templates using the U-Coins or withdraw to real money. If choose to withdraw, we will pay them through PayPal. For the web-hosting function, we are planning to find a trustworthy hosting provider to host our website and our customer's sites too. For our security, we will only choose web host providers that offer HTTPS, if not we will request an SSL/TLS certificate from Certificate Authorities and install by ourselves. Last but not least is our AI tools. This is a very challenging one, due to lacks of time and knowledge, we are currently still in research. We are trying our best to figure out how this type of AI work in similar websites, so that we can apply the same concept.
