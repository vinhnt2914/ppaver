Project Description

Overview

Topic

Initially, our idea was to conduct research on GUI. This was our first step in the big picture. By completing our first milestone, we have gathered enough knowledge, therefore we decided to upgrade this topic one step further. Our goal is now to build a website that generates free UI for users. This is a difficult project that requires skills in diverse areas. Acknowledged that we only focus on building the outer face of the website, while learning and improving ourselves so that in the future time, we can give the website the functionalities that we wanted.

Motivation

We are now living in a rapidly changing world. Local shopping is something that belongs to the past. A study conducted by Google has shown that 66% of shoppers prefer online shopping to find items they’re looking for, compared to 27% who prefer offline. We believe that this difference will increase in the following years. As a result, the demand for websites and applications is now surprisingly high. However, it’s not easy to make a website. U need to know how to code and have experience in User Experience. Not everyone has the time for that. Therefore, they seek solutions online. There are already multiple UI or website-making companies on the market. However, they won’t give you everything for free, there is always a limitation, most commonly known are free trials, and limits on aspects like your web bandwidth or a custom domain. That’s why this project of ours will be completely free. Not only do we provide users with solutions to their problems, but this is also a chance for us to practice and improve our skills such as coding, advertising, team working, and to boost up our Portfolio.

Roles

From our perspective, defining roles for particular participants is crucially important, which helps the project take less time to be done, and improves the productivity of the group project. Moreover, each role is critical to the overall success and operations of the group project, better time management, and identifies areas of weakness that eliminate all unhealthy competition or conflicts between members. Before defining roles and responsibilities, we first need to clearly understand each member's characteristics. After considering it, we decided to choose Vinh as the leader and the Main Developer because he has a profound understanding of coding and every member’s strengths and weaknesses and will organize and guide the team by making decisions that will benefit the project. Continue with Thai as our Graphic User Design who designed our website that looks better to the users, he always enhances the communication between members when struggling or having some personal problem, and provides recommendations. Another person is Thanh Mai, who works as a secretary and constantly notes down important information and issues that our group is struggling with and brings up a solution. She also keeps checking the work, making sure everything is on track. Last but not least, Kien takes on the research part, collects, reports, and analyzes the data, and provides critical information that is needed for the project. Besides, he is willing to support any members that are struggling with their part and make sure the project follows the requirements and standards. Our group does not change roles throughout the working period because changing roles will interrupt the process which takes more time and effort, create more broken parts and make it more complicated. Therefore, we will do the best we can based on each individual’s strengths with the spirit of mutual support.
